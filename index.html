Biography

I am a Ph.D student in the field of Statistics at Cornell University supervised by Prof. Yang Ning. My research interests span widely on statistics, machine learning and optimization. Currently, I am particularly interested in the computational challenges in statistical settings.

Before my Ph.D, I received dual Bachelor’s degrees from Cornell University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I feel fortunate to graduate from Chengdu No.7 high school. Apart from my research, I am a clarinetist and basketball freak.

Interests

  • Machine Learning Theory
  • Non-convex Optimization
